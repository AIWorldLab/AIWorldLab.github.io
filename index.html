About Visual World Model

A visual world model is a system that understands and simulates the laws of the physical world through visual observation and conditioned interaction.

Our Research Focus

Our lab is currently focused on several key challenges within VWMs:

  • Representing from Observation: The Perceptual Foundation of VWM
  • Learning the Rules of the World: The Cognitive Core of VWM
  • Simulating the Future through Interaction: The Key Capability of VWM
